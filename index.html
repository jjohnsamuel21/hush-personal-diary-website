Security & Privacy

Your diary. Your keys. Your rules.

Hush was built with the assumption that your private thoughts should stay private. Every note is encrypted before it touches storage — no server, no sync, no exposure.

  • AES-256-GCM encryption per entry, on-device
  • Biometric unlock — Face ID & fingerprint
  • PIN protection with custom lock timeout
  • Offline-first — works with no internet, forever
  • Encrypted export backup — take your data anywhere
  • Zero tracking, zero analytics, zero ads
🔐

On-device only

Private diary entries are AES-256-GCM encrypted locally. The encryption key never leaves your phone.

🫆

Biometric + PIN

Unlock with your fingerprint, face, or a custom PIN. Auto-locks after a configurable timeout.

📦

Encrypted export

Export your entire diary as an encrypted ZIP backup. Import it on any device — your data, portable.

The Editor

Write the way you think

A distraction-free editor with the richness of a desktop app. Format deeply, or write plainly — Hush adapts to how your thoughts flow.

  • Rich text — bold, italic, headings, lists, quotes
  • 5 typefaces: Merriweather, DM Serif, Caveat, Lato, DM Sans

Write together, privately
